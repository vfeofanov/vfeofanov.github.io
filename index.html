Hello! I'm Vasilii

PhD Candidate in Machine Learning.

Since October 2018, I am a phd student at the Université Grenoble Alpes, doing my research in the AMA team of the Laboratoire d'Informatique de Grenoble (LIG). My thesis work is devoted to study of partially labeled data in the multi-class high-dimensional setting. Both theotetical and applied issues are inviting to me. I am supervised by Emilie Devijver and Massih-Reza Amini.

I graduated the Université Grenoble Alpes by obtaining a Master 2 degree in Applied Mathematics and a Master 1 degree in Computer Science. Before that, I obtained a Bachelor degree in Applied Mathematics and Computer Science at the St. Petersburg State University.
