Hello! I'm Vasilii,

PhD in Machine Learning.

Since January 2022, I have been working as a Senior Research Scientist/Engineer at the Paris Noah's Ark Lab, where I am a member of the Time Series Research team. My current research focuses on transformers for time series, unsupervised analysis of pre-trained models, and self-training algorithms. Previously, I completed my PhD under the supervision of Massih-Reza Amini and Emilie Devijver, where I explored both the theoretical and practical aspects of semi-supervised learning.
